## Minimal Processing Summary (GDPR Art. 12)

- Account & Authentication — Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR (contract)
  - Data: email, password hash (no plaintext), user ID, timestamps
  - Purpose: registration, login, account management, session handling
- App Operation/Telemetry — Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interests)
  - Data: technical usage data (e.g., app version, device/OS, event timestamps, request IDs) with PII minimization/redaction
  - Purpose: availability, security, troubleshooting, service quality
- Optional Device Identifiers — Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR (consent)
  - Data: device ID or similar identifier (only after explicit opt‑in)
  - Purpose: conflict resolution/diagnostics for specific features; revocable at any time
- Preferences/Content — Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR; optionally Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR
  - Data: favorites, playlists, language, in‑app settings
  - Purpose: personalized features, remembering choices
- Optional health‑related self‑tracking data — Art. 9(2)(a) GDPR (explicit consent)
  - Data: only what you actively enter; local storage with optional EU sync
  - Purpose: specific in‑app functions; disabled without consent

For full details (recipients, storage periods, international transfers, and your rights), please consult the online policy when available.
